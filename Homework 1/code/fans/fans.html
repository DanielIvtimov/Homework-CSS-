Supporter Relations

Daniel Finkelstein: We are here to make our fans proud

As a life-long Chelsea fan, I am committed to working closely with supporters to ensure their views are represented at all levels. There are many ways you can connect with Chelsea FC and I would encourage you to get in touch with us to play your part and make your voice heard.

Fan Advisory Board

The Fan Advisory Board (the FAB) facilitates in-depth consultation between the club and a group of fan representatives on strategic matters affecting the club and fans. It ensures fans’ perspectives are heard at the highest levels of the club and fan sentiment plays a key role in all decision making.
